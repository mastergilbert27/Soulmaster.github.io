Dr. FELLOMENO A. GARGAR

                 Dr. Felomino A. Gargar, the Vice President for Academic Affairs of the Holy Child Colleges of Butuan, Butuan City was awarded as the 2012 Lily Rosqueta Rosales Counselor Leadership Awardee in a solemn ceremony at Central Colleges of the Philippines, Manila during the National Conference and Seminar-Workshop with the theme “Revitalizing The Pursuit of Guidance Excellence : Integrating Gender Issues And Roles” last May 3, 2012. The awardee has a very impressive academic credentials, very extensive practice in counseling being the Co-Founder of the St. Peter’s Counseling Home in Davao City, very expansive community outreach activities and a deep commitment to the profession being an advocate, academician and leader. He is one of the eight (8) life members of the Iota Phi-Chi Sigma Iota Counseling Academic and Professional Honor Society International, an accredited psychologist of the Philippine National Police and a licensed guidance counselor.              HCCB ACADEMIC PROGRAMS UNDERGOING ACCREDITATION

Elementary and High School programs, Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED), Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSED), Bachelor of Science and Business Administration (BSBA) and Bachelor of Science in Criminology were submitted for PACUCOA Consultancy Visit These programs are now prepared for Candidate Status for PACUCOA accreditation on February 2014.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT), Bachelor of Science in Hotel and Restaurant Management (BSHRM), Bachelor of Science in Tourism (BS Tour), Bachelor of Science in Midwifery and Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) are scheduled for consultancy visit by May 2014.

All programs are submitted to the Philippine Association of Colleges and Universities Commission on Accreditation (PACUCOA) for accreditation. It is projected that by 2015 all programs are already level II accredited.
